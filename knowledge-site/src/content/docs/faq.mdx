## Merchants

### New Merchants
<details>
    <summary>How do I register as a new merchant?</summary>
    Complete your sign up through the Onboarding registration page [here](https://onboard.Lean.x.io/register) and upload your required documents. Refer to the [registration steps](/registration-steps/) for an in-depth step-by-step-guide during your registration.
</details>

<details>
    <summary>What documents are required for merchant registration?</summary>
    1. A soft copy of your company&apos;s SSM certification
    2. A soft copy of the director&apos;s identification card
    3. A soft copy of your latest bank statement
</details>

<details>
    <summary>How long does account approval take?</summary>
    Account approval usually takes 3 to 4 working days. If you have **not** heard from us for longer than that, please reach out to us! 
</details>

<details>
    <summary>Can I register as an individual or do I need a company?</summary>
    You will need to have a registered company (either Enterprise or Sdn. Bhd.) as we only make settlements to company bank accounts.
</details> 

<details>
    <summary>Is there a monthly or annual maintenance fee?</summary>
    There are no monthly or annual fees for both Lite and Growth package subscribers. To see more info on the fees and pricing, click [here](/packages).
</details> 

### Existing Merchants

    content
</details> 

<details>
    <summary>Can I have multiple merchant accounts under one business?</summary>
    No, each business can only register for 1 merchant account.
</details> 

<details>
    <summary>I forgot my password, what do I do? </summary>
    <Steps>
        1. **Access Forgot Password Page**
           
           Click on "Forgot Password" at the [Login](https://portal.leanpay.my/login) page. Alternatively, you may also click [here](https://portal.leanpay.my/forgot-password) to access the page directly.

        2. **Enter Email**

           This must be the email you used to register your account.

        3. **Send the Form**

           The send button is at the end of the form. After sending, you will receive a recovery link in your email.

        4. **Open Recovery Email**
           
           Open the email and follow the instructions as stated to set a new password.
    </Steps>

    :::tip
    If you do not receive the recovery email within a few minutes, check your spam/junk folder or [contact our support team](/contact-us/).
    :::
</details> 

## Transactions
<details>
    <summary>Can my customers pay from their mobile devices?</summary>
    Yes, your customers can make payments directly from their mobile devices.
</details> 

<details>
    <summary>Why is my payment showing as “Pending”?</summary>
    A payment may show as Pending because the transaction has not yet received confirmation from PayNet. Pending transactions usually require manual checking. The final status will be updated automatically within 1 hour.
</details> 

<details>
    <summary>While making payment using FPX, I received an error or unsuccessful message. However money has been deducted. What should I do?</summary>
    If the payment was unsuccessful but the amount was deducted, the transaction will usually be reversed automatically by your bank. Please allow up to 24&ndash;48 hours for the refund to reflect in your account. If the refund does not appear after this time, kindly contact your bank directly for further assistance.
</details> 

<details>
    <summary>Can I accept international payments?</summary>
    Yes, {branding.leanx} supports purchases and payments from customers outside Malaysia, as long as the transaction is made using a VISA or Mastercard.
</details>
