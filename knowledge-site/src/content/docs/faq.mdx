## Onboarding & Account Issues

<details>
    <summary>How do I register as a new merchant?</summary>
    Complete your sign up through the Onboarding registration page [here](https://onboard.Lean.x.io/register) and upload your required documents. Refer to the [registration steps](/registration-steps/) for an in-depth step-by-step-guide during your registration.
</details>

<details>
    <summary>What documents are required for merchant registration?</summary>
    1. A soft copy of your company&apos;s SSM certification
    2. A soft copy of the director&apos;s identification card
    3. A soft copy of your latest bank statement
</details>

<details>
    <summary>How long does account approval take?</summary>
    Account approval usually takes 3 to 4 working days. If you have **not** heard from us for longer than that, please reach out to us! 
</details>

<details>
    <summary>Can I register as an individual or do I need a company?</summary>
    You will need to have a registered company (either Enterprise or Sdn. Bhd.) as we only make settlements to company bank accounts.
</details>

<details>
    <summary>I forgot my password, what do I do? </summary>
    <Steps>
        1. **Access Forgot Password Page**
           
           Click on "Forgot Password" at the [Login](https://portal.leanpay.my/login) page. Alternatively, you may also click [here](https://portal.leanpay.my/forgot-password) to access the page directly.

        2. **Enter Email**

           This must be the email you used to register your account.

        3. **Send the Form**

           The send button is at the end of the form. After sending, you will receive a recovery link in your email.

        4. **Open Recovery Email**
           
           Open the email and follow the instructions as stated to set a new password.
    </Steps>

    :::tip
    If you do not receive the recovery email within a few minutes, check your spam/junk folder or [contact our support team](/contact-us/).
    :::
</details>
